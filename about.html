Welcome to God’s Heritage

“Children are a heritage from the Lord, offspring a reward from Him.” – Psalm 127:3

God’s Heritage, is an initiative born from the desire to train up children in a godly manner, so they can grow into excellent individuals who thrive in life and impact the world for God’s glory. Children are God's gift, mighty arrows of impact and change. Therefore, it is key how we nurture and guide such precious gifts from the Lord. Through exposing children to the word of God, God's Heritage is training up children in a godly manner that they may be excellent individuals thriving and impacting the community for God.

This website includes:

Bible Stories in Audio Diaries

The word of God is how the world is frame (Hebrews 11:3). These audio diaries bring Scripture to life through storytelling that children can listen to and connect with anytime. Designed for ages 8 and above

Fun & Engaging Activities

Creative activities that help children not only hear Bible stories but also experience them in meaningful ways whilst building critical thinking and analytical skills. Available in different age ranges from 3 and above.

Comics & Storybooks (Coming Soon!)

Visual and interactive resources that help children learn how to live out biblical truths in their everyday lives. Through engaging storytelling with relatable characters their age, children can see their faith in action. Our comics and books are designed for different age ranges, starting from 5 and above.
